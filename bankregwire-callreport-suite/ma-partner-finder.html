M&A partner finder

Screen the field for a fit_

Pick a bank, choose whether it is looking for targets or acquirers, and screen the FDIC universe in its market for candidates ranked by a transparent fit model: size compatibility, geographic overlap, deposit-franchise complementarity, and financial health. This is a directional screen, not deal advice; charter, ownership, culture, and price decide real transactions.

>
Examples
Source: FDIC BankFind Suite API. The screen pulls active institutions in the base bank's state plus adjacent states within a size band, then ranks by a weighted fit score computed in-browser. Field codes use candidate fallbacks; if a metric is blank, that bank lacked the field. Untested against the live API from the build environment; if the browser blocks the request, proxy via your Cloudflare Worker. Geographic fit uses state adjacency only, not MSA overlap. A real M&A screen also weighs charter type, holding-company structure, ownership, deposit-market concentration (HHI/antitrust), interest-rate marks, and CRE concentration.
Screening and education tool. Not investment-banking, legal, or fairness-opinion advice.
