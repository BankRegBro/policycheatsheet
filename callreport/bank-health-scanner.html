Bank health scanner

Pull a bank, read the trend_

Search any FDIC-insured bank and pull its last eight quarters of Call Report financials live, organized by CAMELS component with quarter-over-quarter and year-over-year deltas, sparklines, and threshold flags. Data comes from the FDIC's public API; figures are quarterly and post roughly 30 to 60 days after quarter-end.

Source: FDIC BankFind Suite API (banks.data.fdic.gov), public, no key required. Dollar values reported in thousands; ratios are FDIC-computed annualized figures unless noted. Quarterly data; restatements are reflected as banks amend.
Field mapping was built against the FDIC RISVIEW dictionary and may need a code adjusted on first run; the FIELD_MAP block at the top of the script is the single place to edit. If the browser blocks the request (CORS), route it through your Cloudflare Worker as a proxy. Sensitivity (S) is only partly covered by FDIC standardized fields; the HTM unrealized-loss tell needs the FFIEC CDR extract.
