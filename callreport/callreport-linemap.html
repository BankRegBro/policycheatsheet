The one-screen supervisory read

CAN IT ABSORB LOSSES?RC-R: CET1, Tier 1 leverage, and total risk-based ratios against RWA tell you the cushion and the distance to PCA thresholds.
ARE LOSSES COMING?RC-N and RI-B: past-due and nonaccrual balances show deterioration before it hits earnings; the ACL coverage ratio shows whether reserves keep pace.
CAN IT EARN ITS WAY OUT?RI: net interest income, provision, and the efficiency ratio flow down to ROA and ROE.
WILL LIQUIDITY FORCE THE ISSUE?RC securities and deposit lines: the gap between HTM carrying value and fair value, plus funding mix, decides whether a runoff turns unrealized losses into realized ones.
