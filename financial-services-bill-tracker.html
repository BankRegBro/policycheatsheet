About this dispatch. A working catalogue of financial-services legislation in the 119th Congress, maintained by a practitioner across state and federal supervision. Each entry is keyed to its primary source on Congress.gov; status reflects the most recent recorded action as of the revision date. Omnibus vehicles — chiefly the Main Street Capital Access Act — are flagged because several stand-alone bills here also travel inside them.
Read with care. Legislative posture changes on the wire; verify against the live Congress.gov record before relying on any item. Companion measures, substitute amendments, and committee-reported variants may differ materially from introduced text. Not legal advice.
