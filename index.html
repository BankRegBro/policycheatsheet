About
the wire

BankRegWire is a working catalogue of U.S. financial services regulation, maintained by a practitioner across state and federal supervision. Each dispatch distills a specific rule, piece of guidance, piece of legislation, or structural feature of the dual banking system into a single working document. Methodology favors statute and rule text over secondary commentary; conclusions are front-loaded. Dispatches are revised on the wire as agency actions and legislative posture evolve. Corrections and comments: feedback@bankregwire.com.
